Case Study 2024

OrbitWatch: Space Education App

Bridging the Gap Between Data and the Stars. An engineering deep-dive into real-time orbital mechanics and atmospheric visibility logic.

Stack
SwiftUI Core Location Metal
Vision & Origin

The Why

Stemming from a rigorous STEM background, the objective was to democratize complex orbital data. We envisioned a tool that transforms raw TLE (Two-Line Element) sets into intuitive, visual experiences for the next generation of engineers.

The Inspiration

Rooted in the nostalgia of late-night ISS tracking, the project was born from a desire to recreate that sense of wonder using modern cross-platform architecture and real-time data streaming.

Core Features & Technical Highlights
🛰️
01 / Live Tracking

Real-Time ISS Tracking

Low-latency polling of International Space Station coordinates via REST API, mapped onto a custom Metal-rendered 3D globe with sub-second precision.

🌌

NASA APOD Integration

Daily orbital imagery fetched via official NASA Open APIs, with cached image handling to prevent flickering and skeleton loading states.

📍

Flyover Alerts

Geofencing logic that calculates the intersection of user coordinates and satellite orbital planes for pass predictions.

🏗️

Cross-Platform Architecture

Built with a modular Swift core, allowing for shared business logic between iOS and watchOS, ensuring high performance across the Apple ecosystem.
